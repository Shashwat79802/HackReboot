Welcome to BITDClubs

About

HackClubBitDurg is a developer-centric community with our core values aligning with diversity and inclusivity in Tech and related domains. We aim at creating a culture that promotes learning in collaboration and growth by implementation. The community welcomes all - to contribute, participate, network, and most importantly have fun while embarking on this exciting journey of personal growth.

 

BITDClubs is a platform that provides details about the clubs and communities on the campus and establishes a quick communication channel between the students and the organizers. The site gives you complete information about the club, team structure, upcoming events, past-events,etc.


The project is live on GitHub you can check it out, raise issues or contribute by solving current ones!! Contributions in the form of code, content, design, and creativity are most welcome. You are just one PR away from making this initiative a part of your work experience!!
